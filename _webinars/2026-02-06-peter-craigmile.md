## Abstract

Methods of estimation and forecasting for stationary models are well known in classical time series analysis. However, stationarity is an idealization which, in practice, can at best hold as an approximation.

## Speaker Bio

Peter F. Craigmile is a Professor in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Hunter College, CUNY. His research interests include time series and longitudinal analysis, spatial statistics, and related methodology.

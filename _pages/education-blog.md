The Time Series Learning Blog makes time series research more accessible through student perspectives. Written for students, practitioners, and researchers new to featured webinar topics, posts provide clear explanations, brief literature context, and resources for further learning.

UConn students have the opportunity to post writing such as a literature review, summary, or tutorial based on webinar talks. If you are interested in learning, writing, and helping make seminar topics accessible, email [timeseriesconnect@gmail.com](mailto:timeseriesconnect@gmail.com).

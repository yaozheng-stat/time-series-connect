## Time Series Education Blog

We aim to make time series research more approachable through student perspectives. The Time Series Learning Blog is intended for readers of all backgrounds: students, practitioners, and researchers who may be new to the specific topics featured in our webinars.

Posts are created by students and aim to provide accessible explanations, brief literature context, and pointers for further learning.

UConn students have the opportunity to post writing such as a literature review, summary, or tutorial based on webinar talks. If you are interested in learning, writing, and helping make seminar topics accessible, email [timeseriesconnect@gmail.com](mailto:timeseriesconnect@gmail.com).
